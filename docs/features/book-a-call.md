# Feature: Book a Call

## Context

A contact form creates friction — it feels like emailing into the void with no clear next step. A calendar booking link (Calendly or equivalent) lets high-intent visitors take a concrete action immediately: book a 30-minute discovery call. Higher-intent leads, faster pipeline.

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Add a "Book a free discovery call" CTA alongside or above the contact form
- Integrate a Calendly (or equivalent) link — opens in a new tab or embedded widget
- Update primary CTAs on homepage hero and services page to point to this
- Keep the contact form as a secondary option for people who prefer async

**Out of scope:**
- Building a custom booking system

## Open Questions

- Calendly free tier (limited features) or a paid alternative (Cal.com self-hosted, TidyCal)?
- Embed the calendar widget inline on the contact/hire page, or link out to Calendly?
- What to call the session: "discovery call", "intro call", "free consultation"?
