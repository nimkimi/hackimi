# Feature: Process / How I Work Page

## Context

Small businesses are often nervous about hiring a solo dev — fear of being ghosted mid-project, overcharged, or getting something unusable. A transparent "how I work" page removes this anxiety before the first contact. It acts as a pre-sales tool that answers the unspoken question: "What happens after I reach out?"

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Describe the engagement steps: discovery call → proposal → design/build → review cycles → handoff + optional support
- Include what the client is responsible for at each step (content, feedback windows, etc.)
- Clear, non-technical language aimed at business owners

**Out of scope:**
- Legal/contract details
- Pricing specifics

## Open Questions

- Standalone `/process` page or a section embedded within the services page? (Recommendation: section within services, linked from the nav)
- Visual format: numbered steps timeline, or plain text with icons?
